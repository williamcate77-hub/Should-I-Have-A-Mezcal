Should I Have a Mezcal?

A tiny mezcal decision helper you can save like an app.

Should I Have a Mezcal is a tiny web app for curiosity and late-night decision making. Tap the button and let the mezcal gods decide.

How it works

It keeps things simple: press the button and see what the mezcal gods say.

Save to your home screen on iPhone or iPad

Safari on iOS

  • Open the Mezcal app using the button below in Safari.
  • Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen.
  • Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Rename it if you like, then tap Add.

Save to your home screen on Android

Chrome on Android

  • Open the Mezcal app using the button below in Chrome.
  • Tap the three dot menu in the top right corner.
  • Tap Add to Home screen or Install app.
  • Confirm the name, then tap Add.
